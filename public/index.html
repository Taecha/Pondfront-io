How to Play

Pond control basics

Basics

Expand into neutral pond tiles and grow Animal Energy from territory.

Animals

Duck expands fast, Snake controls reeds and mud, Frog jumps, Turtle defends, and Carp scales economy.

Combat

Attack connected enemy borders with energy waves and reinforce your own fronts.

Buildings

Build farms, nests, guards, tunnels, and jump pads to shape your economy.

Diplomacy

Request alliances, mark enemies, send signals, and break deals when the lake changes.

Winning

Eliminate rivals until one animal or team remains. Sandbox ending is off unless enabled.

    Quick Start

    Be the last animal standing

    1. Click a glowing neutral border tile, then Expand.
    2. Animal Energy grows with territory and special zones.
    3. Use Bite, Push, Wave, or Max only on connected enemy borders.
    4. Defend border tiles and build farms, nests, and guards.
    5. Capture objectives, use specials carefully, and protect your Core Nest.
    6. Control the pond or outlast the other animals to win.
