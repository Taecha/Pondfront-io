How to Play

Pond control basics

Basics

Expand into neutral pond tiles and grow Animal Energy from territory.

Animals

Duck expands fast, Snake controls reeds and mud, Frog jumps, Turtle defends, and Carp scales economy.

Combat

Attack connected enemy borders with energy waves and reinforce your own fronts.

Buildings

Build farms, nests, guards, tunnels, and jump pads to shape your economy.

Diplomacy

Request alliances, mark enemies, send signals, and break deals when the lake changes.

Winning

Eliminate rivals until one animal or team remains. Sandbox ending is off unless enabled.

      Welcome to PondFront.io

      Be the last animal standing

      Choose an animal, grow your habitat, manage energy, and use your unique ability to become the final surviving faction.

      1. Your selected animal has a passive perk and one active ability.
      2. Select a glowing neutral border tile and press Expand.
      3. The preview shows how much Animal Energy the expansion spends.
      4. Energy regenerates from territory and income tiles.
      5. Build a Lily Farm on a valid owned tile for more income.
      6. Only connected border tiles can expand or attack.
      7. Select a weak enemy border and start with a small Bite.
      8. Use your animal ability when its cooldown is ready.
      9. Select another player to request peace or an alliance.
      10. Complete the mode objective or remain the final faction to win.

      Coach hints will continue on the map and react to your next action.
