How to Play

Pond control basics

Basics

Expand into neutral pond tiles and grow Animal Energy from territory.

Animals

Duck expands fast, Snake controls reeds and mud, Frog jumps, Turtle defends, and Carp scales economy.

Combat

Attack connected enemy borders with energy waves and reinforce your own fronts.

Buildings

Build farms, nests, guards, tunnels, and jump pads to shape your economy.

Diplomacy

Request alliances, mark enemies, send signals, and break deals when the lake changes.

Winning

Control 70% of the playable lake, or lead the territory count when time runs out.
