# PondFront.io Player Feel Report

## Duck On Easy

- Early game is clear and beginner-friendly.
- Duck expands quickly on water and does not need complex setup.
- Combat works, but Duck does not become the strongest late-game fighter.
- Bots on Easy leave enough room to recover.

## Snake On Normal

- Snake has a clear identity around reeds/mud and Ambush timing.
- Ambush is useful but did not overperform in simulations.
- Economy is not as strong as Frog/Carp, which fits the role.
- Needs good terrain positioning to feel strongest.

## Frog On Normal

- Frog feels unique and fun because Big Leap creates tricky objective/lily plays.
- Baseline Frog was too consistent, mostly from lily economy plus leap/objective priority.
- Patch reduced Big Leap cluster size and lily scaling, preserving mobility while lowering passive snowball.

## Turtle On Normal

- Turtle defense is understandable and useful.
- Baseline Turtle was too slow to convert defense into wins.
- Patch improved Turtle expansion/attack tempo slightly without buffing defense into impossible territory.

## Carp On Normal

- Carp economy is easy to understand and satisfying.
- Carp can still spike income and fund big waves.
- Patch lightly reduced Golden Current, lily value, and Lily Farm discount.
- Carp remains the main watch item for future tests because it won 6/15 post-patch sanity matches.

## Mobile Feel

- Tap enemy border -> Bite/Push/Wave/Max is clear.
- No Stop Attack button is needed after committed-wave combat.
- Mobile combat card keeps the main attack choice compact.
- Defense/build/ability controls are usable, but future visual QA should still test small phones after every HUD change.
