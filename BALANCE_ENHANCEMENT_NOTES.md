# PondFront.io Balance Enhancement Notes

Date: 2026-07-05

## Goals

- Keep Easy and Normal bots fair while making bot matches feel alive.
- Reduce economy snowballing from Carp and Lily Farms.
- Keep Turtle durable but not impossible to break.
- Keep Snake Ambush strong without making one attack feel unfair.
- Keep Lily Barrage useful but not match-ending.

## Buffs

- Dragonfly Guard cost reduced from 90 to 85 Animal Energy.
- Dragonfly Guard duration increased from 20s to 22s.
- Normal bots received a tiny attack consistency nudge: aggression 0.48 and attack percent multiplier 0.74.
- Bots now have a midgame skirmish fallback, so quiet matches produce more border fighting without making Easy bots dogpile the player.

## Nerfs

- Lily Farm base income reduced from 0.78 to 0.72.
- Lily Farm lily bonus reduced from 0.18 to 0.16.
- Lily Farm frog bonus reduced from 0.14 to 0.12.
- Lily Farm soft-cap penalty increased from 0.08 to 0.10.
- Carp water income bonus reduced from 0.012 to 0.010.
- Carp lily bonus reduced from 0.105 to 0.095.
- Golden Current income multiplier reduced from 0.24 to 0.22.
- Turtle border defense multiplier reduced from 1.14 to 1.12.
- Duck Flock Rush open-water capture discount softened from 0.65 to 0.68.
- Snake Ambush attack power multiplier reduced from 1.55 to 1.48.
- Lily Barrage max captures reduced from 4 to 3.
- Lily Barrage base pressure reduced from 42 to 38.

## Bot Fairness

- Bot surrender now requires recent pressure, core loss, a recent war, or a very late-game state.
- Easy bot skirmish chance is intentionally tiny and avoids targeting the player unless the player attacked first.
- Normal bots should still expand slower than strong human play, but they now fight more reliably after contact.
- Hard and Chaos bots benefit most from the skirmish fallback, which fits their intended difficulty.

## Expected Results

- Early expansion remains smooth for the player.
- Carp and Lily Farm still feel rewarding but should snowball less sharply.
- Turtle remains defensive but not locked forever.
- Snake Ambush remains scary in reeds/mud while leaving more counterplay.
- Lily Barrage is now more of a weakening tool than a tile wipe.
- Bot matches should include expansion, building, ability use, diplomacy, specials, and normal attacks.
