# PondFront.io Next Ideas

## Best Next Upgrade

Add stronger objective-driven late game. The game works, bots fight, and abilities are useful, but matches can still end by timer without a dominant winner. Objectives should become the reason players clash.

## High-Value Ideas

- Objective surges: after 5 minutes, active objectives pulse stronger bonuses and announce contested borders.
- Border war panel: show who is attacking you, recent tiles lost, and one-tap "Defend Front".
- Attack clarity: show "Captured 4 tiles", "Stopped by defense", and "Too weak" directly on the map.
- Animal mastery: small match goals for each animal, such as Duck water chain, Snake ambush capture, Frog leap objective.
- Better end screen: include strongest rival, favorite terrain, biggest comeback, and most valuable objective.

## Later Ideas

- Private practice presets: Beginner Pond, Bot War, Objective Rush, Huge Lake Chaos.
- Cosmetic pond themes that do not change gameplay.
- Replay timeline for attacks, alliances, objectives, and final rank changes.
