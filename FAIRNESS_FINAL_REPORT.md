# PondFront.io Fairness Final Report

## Final Animal Balance

- Duck: fair beginner animal. Fast expansion, not dominant in win rate.
- Snake: fair combat animal. Ambush remains dangerous but not meta-breaking.
- Frog: nerfed from overperforming. Still mobile/objective-focused, less passive lily snowball.
- Turtle: buffed slightly. Better tempo while staying killable.
- Carp: still strong economy identity. Lightly nerfed; remains a watch item.

## Final Combat Balance

- Bite/Push/Wave/Max attacks are useful and server-authoritative.
- Small attacks weaken or capture.
- Push is the normal useful attack.
- Wave and Max are strong but risky because energy is committed up front.
- Defense still matters through terrain, reinforcement, Turtle bonuses, Reed Guard, and counter-attacks.
- Counter-attacks can create contested waves.

## Final Economy Balance

- Lily Farm spam is slightly slower and more expensive.
- Carp economy is toned down without removing its identity.
- Max energy and income still scale with territory, so map control remains important.
- Large-map building totals improved in post-patch sanity, from 219.8 average buildings to 201.8.

## Final Bot Difficulty Balance

- Easy bots remain beginner-friendly.
- Normal bots are fair but not perfect.
- Hard bots are smarter without direct cheating.
- Chaos bots keep higher aggression.
- Bot defense mistake settings remain in place.
- Bot Current Push is more visible but still rare.

## Remaining Risks

- Carp won 6/15 in the smaller post-patch sanity sample. This may be variance, but it should be watched in the next 30-match run.
- Large-map matches often reach the time cap in accelerated sims, so late-game ending pressure may need a future pass.
- Current Push still did not appear on Medium/Large in the post-patch sample; route availability may be the limiting factor, not just chance.
- Mobile visual QA should be repeated on real small-screen dimensions after every HUD change.

## Recommended Next Balance Test

- Run another 30-match bot simulation after more playtesting.
- Watch Carp win rate, Frog objective control, and Turtle large-map survival.
- Play 3 human matches on Normal: Duck, Turtle, Carp.
- Specifically test Current Push on Medium maps with water-heavy borders.
